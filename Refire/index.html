REFIRE Supports Hydrogen Technology Markets Across The Globe

We are active in upstream markets, transforming electricity into hydrogen (E2H) and in downstream markets, transforming hydrogen back into electricity (H2E). We build products and ecosystems that allow renewable sources of electricity to be stored as green hydrogen, and for that hydrogen to then power zero emissions mobility and power applications.
